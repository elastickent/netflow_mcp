# Natural Language NetFlow MCP Questions

## Traffic Analysis Questions
- What's the current network traffic like?
- Show me the top bandwidth consumers in the last hour
- Which services are using the most bandwidth?
- What's the traffic trend over the past 30 minutes?
- Who are the top talkers on the network?
- What countries are we exchanging traffic with?
- Which ports are most active right now?

## Bottleneck Detection Questions
- Are there any network bottlenecks?
- Which interfaces are overutilized?
- Is the WAN link saturated?
- Show me interfaces above 70% utilization
- Who's consuming bandwidth on the DMZ interface?
- What's causing high traffic on interface 1?

## Security/Anomaly Questions
- Are there any security concerns right now?
- Is anyone port scanning our network?
- Detect any unusual traffic patterns
- Are there any traffic spikes or drops?
- Which hosts are accessing unusual ports?
- Find suspicious network activity

## Host Investigation Questions
- What's 192.168.1.100 doing on the network?
- Show me all traffic from 10.0.0.50
- Who is talking to 172.16.1.10?
- What services is 192.168.1.25 accessing?
- Investigate traffic to and from 10.1.1.87
- Which hosts are connecting to this IP address?

## Recommendation Questions
- What should I focus on right now?
- Give me network recommendations
- What actions should I take to improve network performance?
- Are there any critical issues I should address?
- What's the network health status?

## Combined/Complex Questions
- Is host 192.168.1.50 causing the bottleneck on WAN-1?
- Find the source of the traffic spike in the last 15 minutes
- Which internal hosts are communicating with external IPs on non-standard ports?
- Show me potential DDoS activity
- What's consuming bandwidth during business hours?
